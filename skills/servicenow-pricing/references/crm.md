# CRM & Industry Workflows Pricing & Packaging

## Overview
CRM/Industry Workflows include Customer Service Management (CSM), Field Service Management (FSM), CPQ/SOM, and Industry-specific solutions (FSO, TSM, TPSM, HCLS-SM, PSDS, MCO). Licensed by **Fulfiller**, **Unrestricted User**, **Transactions**, or industry-specific meters.

---

## Supported Licensing Models

| Meter | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| Fulfiller | User with rights to use and access application(s) within the procured product |
| Business Stakeholder | User with right to view/approve records or create cases on behalf of customers |
| Requester | User with right to request something for themselves |
| App User | User with right to access one or more custom applications |
| Transactions | Activities in the product (cases, order lines, work orders, portal visits, Assists) |
| Unrestricted User | Size of organization's workforce (all users in sys_user table) |
