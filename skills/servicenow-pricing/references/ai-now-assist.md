# Now Assist & AI Pricing & Packaging

## Overview
Now Assist is ServiceNow's Gen AI solution. Licensed as **add-ons** to Pro & Enterprise base products, metered by **Assists** (usage tokens) pooled across the account.

---

## Licensing Model

### How Now Assist Works
1. **Add-on SKUs**: Sold per-user as add-ons to Pro/Enterprise tiers
2. **Assists**: Each Gen AI action consumes "assists" (usage tokens)
3. **Pooling**: Assists are pooled across all products and instances on the account
4. **Additional Usage**: Assist Packs sold in increments of 500,000 assists

### Key Licensing Rules (Updated Jan 2025)
- **Minimum Quantity**: 25 users for Fulfiller/SPM User/CWM User meters
- **Transaction Meter**: 16,500 minimum
- **Meter Matching**: Can mix meters (e.g., sell Pro Plus on FF meter even when base is UU)
- **Exceptions**: HRSD, H&S, WSD, ITOM, SAM, EA require full Pro/Ent population (Base = Now Assist quantity)

## Key FAQs

### Can customers purchase Pro Plus for a fraction of their users?
- **Fulfiller meter**: Yes, with 25 user minimum
- **UU meter**: No, must match base SKU quantity (with some exceptions)
- **Transaction meter**: Yes, with 16,500 minimum

### Do customers need Azure OpenAI license?
No. The cost of external LLM usage is included in Pro Plus pricing.

### Are assists product-specific?
No. Assists are pooled across all products and instances on the account.

### What happens if customer exceeds assist entitlement?
They can purchase additional Assist Packs ($150K for 500K assists).

### Can CSM customers use ITSM Now Assist features?
Yes. CSM and Industry Pro Plus/Enterprise Plus SKUs entitle customers to use Now Assist for ITSM.

### Pro Plus vs Enterprise Plus difference?
None. Same applications, same assist entitlements, same price. Pro Plus is for Pro base SKUs, Enterprise Plus is for Enterprise base SKUs.
