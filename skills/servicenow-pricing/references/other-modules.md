# Other Modules Pricing & Packaging

## Overview
This reference covers additional ServiceNow products including Risk Management (IRM), Impact, Process Mining, Source-to-Pay, Workflow Data Fabric, and Platform Security.

## Integrated Risk Management (IRM)

### IRM Packaging

#### IRM Standard - $100/Now Assist User/month
**Includes:**
- Policy and Compliance Management
- Risk Management
- Audit Management
- App Engine Starter (5 tables)

#### IRM Professional - $150/Now Assist User/month
**Includes Standard plus:**
- Predictive Intelligence
- Platform Analytics Advanced

#### IRM Enterprise - $200/Now Assist User/month
**Includes Professional plus:**
- Advanced capabilities

### IRM Now Assist

#### IRM Pro/Enterprise Plus - $150/Now Assist User/month
**Alternate Meter:** $12/UU/month
**Assist Entitlement:** 6,000/Now Assist User/year or 600/UU/year

**Included Skills:**
- Control objective impact analyzer
- Issue summarization
- Regulatory alert summarization
- Risk assessment summarization
- Regulatory alert impacted citations
- Regulatory alert impacted policies
- Regulatory mapping with AI (Control Recommendation)
- Recommendation of similar control objectives
- Common control objective creation
