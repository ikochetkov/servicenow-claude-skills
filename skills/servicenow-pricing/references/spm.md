# Strategic Portfolio Management (SPM) Pricing & Packaging

## Overview
Strategic Portfolio Management provides project portfolio management, demand management, resource planning, and agile capabilities. Licensed by **SPM User** or **Unrestricted User**.

## Key Licensing Rules

1. **Minimum Quantities:**
   - SPM User: 25 minimum for Pro Plus
   - CWM User: 50 minimum for standalone, 25 for Pro Plus
   - Business Application: 100 minimum for EA

2. **Meter Restrictions:**
   - UU SKUs are restricted and require BU approval
   - Process Mining SKUs are restricted

3. **Discretion Threshold:**
   - Must remain <30% for Now Assist SKUs

## Digital Portfolio Management (DPM)

### Overview
DPM is a shared capability included in ITSM, SPM/ITBM, and APM/EA licenses at no additional cost.

**Included in:**
- ITSM Standard v1-v3
- ITSM Pro v1-v3
- ITSM Enterprise v1, v3
- SPM Standard
- SPM Professional
- EA (APM) Standard
- EA (APM) Professional

### Licensing
- **Read Access:** DPM users can view data from ITSM, SPM/ITBM, and APM/EA tables
- **Write Access:** Requires license to specific products
