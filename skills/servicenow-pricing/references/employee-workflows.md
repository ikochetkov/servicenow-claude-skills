# Employee Workflows Pricing & Packaging

## Overview
Employee Workflows includes HR Service Delivery (HRSD), Workplace Service Delivery (WSD), Health & Safety, Legal Service Delivery, and Manufacturing Employee Experience. Licensed by **HR User**, **Application User**, or **Unrestricted User**.

---

## HR Service Delivery (HRSD)

### Pricing Models

| Meter | Definition |
|-------|-----------|
| HR User (legacy) | Full-time/part-time employee, contractor, or contingent worker serviced by HR application |
| HR User (new) | Valid record in ServiceNow HR Licensing Table for employee within employment start/end date |
| Unrestricted User | Every active user in sys_user table |

## Migration Guidance

### Migration Percentages

| From | To | Uplift |
|------|-----|--------|
| HR Standard/Service Management/EWF Starter/ESF | HR Standard v3 | 30% |
| HR Professional (all versions) | HR Professional v6 | 50% |
| HR Professional (using LE) | HR Enterprise v6 | 30% |
| HR Enterprise (all versions) | HR Enterprise v6 | 25% |
| Standalone Employee Onboarding | HR Enterprise v6 | 75% |

**Minimum ACV uplift:** 15% for both GenAI and non-GenAI migrations

### Important Notes
- Customers on HRSD Pro using Lifecycle Events should migrate to HRSD Enterprise
- Gen AI migration uses same uplift % as base SKU migration
